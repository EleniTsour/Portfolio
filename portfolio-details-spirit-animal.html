Your Spirit Animal

"Your Spirit Animal" is an interactive web application that offers users a fun and engaging way to discover their spirit animal. The project showcases a collection of captivating images of various animals from around the world. Users can simply press a "Discover" button, and the application will randomly select an image, revealing their unique spirit animal. To further enrich the experience, each displayed animal image is linked to its respective Wikipedia page.
